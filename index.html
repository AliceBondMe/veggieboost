Organic vegetables

Our organic vegetables are hand-picked from local farms and delivered straight to your doorstep, ensuring that you get the freshest and most nutritious produce possible.

  • Carrot

    Plant

    100 UAH / kg

    Carrots are full of benefits—they may promote healthy vision, balance your blood sugar, help with weight management, lower your risk of cancer, regulate blood pressure, reduce heart disease, improve immunity, and boost brain health.

  • Brussel sprouts

    Plant

    80 UAH / kg

    Brussels sprouts are high in fiber, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making them a nutritious addition to your diet. They may also come with added health benefits, including decreased inflammation and improved immune system health.

  • Tomato

    Plant

    40 UAH / kg

    Tomatoes are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. With their high lycopene content, they contribute to a reduced risk of heart disease and cancer. The intake of tomatoes may also improve eye health, digestive health, and reduce risk of osteoporosis.

  • Eggplant

    Plant

    60 UAH / kg

    Eggplants are rich in fiber and antioxidants. A serving of eggplant can provide at least 5% of a person's daily requirement of fiber, copper, manganese, B-6, and thiamine. It also contains other vitamins and minerals. In addition, eggplants are a source of phenolic compounds that act as antioxidants.

  • Sweet potatoes

    Plant

    80 UAH / kg

    Sweet potatoes are high in fiber and antioxidants, which protect your body from free radical damage and promote a healthy gut and brain. They're also incredibly rich in beta carotene, which is converted to vitamin A to support good vision and your immune system.

  • Leek

    Plant

    50 UAH / kg

    Leeks are low in calories but high in nutrients, particularly magnesium and vitamins A, C, and K. They boast small amounts of fiber, copper, vitamin B6, iron, and folate. Leeks are rich in antioxidants and sulfur compounds. These are thought to protect your body from disease.

  • Mushrooms

    Plant

    40 UAH / kg

    Mushrooms are a low-calorie food that packs a nutritional punch. Loaded with many health-boosting vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, they’ve long been recognized as an important part of any diet. The anti-inflammatory effect of mushrooms has been shown to greatly improve the efficiency of the immune system.

  • Corn

    Plant

    60 UAH / kg

    Choosing corn and whole-grain corn products -- rather than food that has processed white flour -- can lead to better gut health and help lower your chances of getting diseases like cancer, heart disease, and type 2 diabetes. The fiber in corn helps you stay full for longer between meals.

  • Radish

    Plant

    90 UAH / kg

    Radishes are a good source of antioxidants like catechin, pyrogallol, vanillic acid, and other phenolic compounds. These root vegetables also have a good amount of vitamin C, which acts as an antioxidant to protect your cells from damage.
